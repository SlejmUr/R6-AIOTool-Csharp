Operation "Shadow Legacy" introduced an operator from another Tom Clancys franchise.
Sam Fisher codenamed Zero was added into the game.
The 1.5x scope, 2.0x scope, and the 3.0X scope were been brought to the game.
Ubisoft also reworked the map Chalet and the first ever operator rework was released.
The Sugar Fright event was available to play during the operation.
